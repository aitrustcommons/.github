# AI Trust Commons

**Open-source governance for AI agents in the enterprise.**

AI agents are deploying faster than governance can keep pace. 80% of Fortune 500 companies have active AI agents — only 14.4% with full security approval. Every cloud vendor publishes governance guidance for their own platform. Nobody covers what happens across providers, protocols, and compliance regimes.

AI Trust Commons builds the cross-provider governance layer the industry is missing — standards-mapped, machine-readable, and open source.

## What We're Building

🔗 **[Governance Framework](https://github.com/aitrustcommons/governance-framework)** — Policy enforcement, audit trails, and compliance mapping for AI agents across AWS, Azure, GCP, and hybrid environments. Maps controls across OWASP Top 10 for Agentic Applications, NIST AI RMF, SOC 2, and EU AI Act.

## Standards Engagement

- **NIST** — Active participant in the AI Agent Standards Initiative (CAISI RFI, NCCoE Identity & Authorization)
- **OWASP** — Contributing to MCP Top 10 and the Agentic Security Initiative
- **EU AI Act** — Article 50 transparency compliance tooling (August 2026 deadline)
